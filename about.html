About Shiva Jan Seva Kendra (CSC)

I am the proud owner and operator of Shiva Jan Seva Kendra, a government-authorized Common Service Center (CSC).

In today's digital age, many people find it difficult to navigate online government portals, banking systems, and documentation. I started this center with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between the government and the common man.

Whether you are a student needing a scholarship form, a farmer needing land records, or a senior citizen needing a pension update, my center is your one-stop solution.

My Core Values
Trust is the most important asset in my line of work.

Honesty (Imandari)

I believe in transparent dealings. No hidden fees, no false promises.

Accuracy

Government forms are sensitive. I ensure every detail is checked twice to avoid rejections.

Service (Seva)

As the name of my center suggests, "Jan Seva" (Public Service) is my priority. I treat every customer with patience and respect.

Rooted in Panuwanaula

Being a local resident, I understand the specific challenges we face in the hills—from network issues to lack of information. Shiva Jan Seva Kendra is not just a shop; it is a community hub. I am committed to bringing the latest digital services to our doorstep so that the people of Panuwanaula do not have to travel to cities for basic work.
