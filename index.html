My Commitment to Service

Serving Panuwanaula with Honesty and Dedication

My Belief

I believe that work done with honesty is always blessed. With the blessings of Mahadev, I try to serve people with a clean heart and clear intentions. For me, helping someone complete their work correctly is more important than anything else.

My Responsibility

As the owner of Shiva Jan Seva Kendra, I understand my responsibility towards society. Many people depend on CSC centers for important government work, so every service must be done carefully and correctly.

Helping People

At my CSC center, I help people with government-related services, online applications, and digital work. I explain the process in a simple way so that people clearly understand what is being done and why it is needed.

Support for All

People from Panuwanaula and nearby villages visit the CSC center daily. Students, farmers, workers, and senior citizens all come here with different needs, and I try to help everyone with patience and respect.

Transparency

I follow a transparent process for every service. Documents are checked properly, details are filled carefully, and correct information is given. This helps avoid mistakes and builds trust with the people who visit the center.

Digital India

Through Shiva Jan Seva Kendra, I try to contribute in a small way towards Digital India. Making digital and government services accessible at the village level helps people save time and effort.

Always Ready to Help

If someone does not understand a process or has doubts, I take time to explain. My CSC center is a place where people can ask questions freely and get the right guidance without fear.

Thank You

I am thankful to everyone who trusts Shiva Jan Seva Kendra and supports my work. This trust motivates me to continue serving people with honesty and dedication.
